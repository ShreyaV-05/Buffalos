Hot spots

Brandenburg Gate

    The Brandenburg Gate is a historical
    site representing the unity between
    east and west berlin after the Cold
    War era.

Berlin Wall

    The Berlin Wall is estimated at 27
    miles wide dividing the east side
    of Berlin from the west. The Berlin
    Wall stands as a historical land
    mark of Germany and is a site to
    not miss out on.

Victory Column

    The Victory Column is a 219ft
    historical statue that commemorates
    the victory in the Danish-Prussian
    War. It is massive and a sight to
    not miss out on.
