Scoma's Restaurant

    Located in Fisherman's Wharf, this restaurant is
    must visit as it's the only restuarant in Pier 39
    that has it's own working 46-foot fishing boat.
    Guests can order freshly caught seafood and enjoy
    their food along with a glass of alcohol in the
    cocktail lounge while watching commercial fishing
    boats in action.
    Price Range:
        $$$$ ($31-$50)
    Special Diets:
        Vegetarian Friendly
        Gluten Free Options
    COVID-19 Regulations:
        Outdoor dining 11:30am - 7:30pm
        To-Go/Curbside Pickup 11:30am - 8:30pm

Barzotto

    Looking for an affordable Italian meal?
    BARZOTTO is here to serve you with the best
    quality meal possible. Located in a homely bar
    in the heart of Mission District, drop by twice
    a week for a glass of wine for $10 or some freshly
    made pasta. Ingrediencts are all sourced from local
    farms in the bay area and pastas are made from
    scratch daily.
    Price Range:
        $$ - $$$
    Hours:
        4-9pm everyday
    COVID-19 Regulations:
        Delivery/To-Go
        Outdoor/Indoor dining

    to satisfy your hunger.
    Price Range:
        $$
    Hours:
        4:30pm - 8:30pm everyday
    COVID-19 Regulations:
        Delivery/To-Go
        Masks required upon visit
