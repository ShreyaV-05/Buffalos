Asian Cuisine

Harney Sushi

Having a sushi bar and restaurant
experience, Harney Sushi is one of
San Diego's top Asian cuisines. It
is SD's only sake bar—a Japanese
alcoholic drink made from fermented
rice that is traditionally drunk
warm in small porcelain cups—and
features 40 award winning wines, 30
amazing sakes, and several gourmet
specialty rolls. This place also has
options that are vegetarian friendly,
vegan, and gluten free for those who
want. The cuisine provides outdoor
as well as indoor seating.

Italian Cuisine

Civico 1845

Civico 1845 brings Italy to San Diego.
Owners Dalio and Pietro Gallo present
a full menu filled with fresh, light,
authentic, Italian dishes. They have
it all, from pastas, to mains, to
antipasti, and best of all, their
seafood, which shines above all. For
those who prefer vegan or gluten free
options, Civico 1845 provides an
extensive selection. This restaurant
serves alcohol for those who are
old enough. There is also outdoor
and indoor seating available.

Mexican Cuisine

Puesto

Puesto is an award-winning Mexican
Artisan Kitchen & Bar is a restaurant
with an innovative approach and filled
with plenty of authentic Mexican
dishes. Puesto is popular for its
crispy melted cheese tacos with
locally sourced ingredients and
handmade non-GMO organic tortillas.
Taco Tuesdays offer discounted prices
that people can take advantage of to
explore a wider variety of their
amazing taco fillings. They have
snacks such as mezcal-pickled
watermelon as well as bigger dishes
of seafood and enchiladas. The
restaurant offers indoor and outdoor
seating, as well as a takout option
