Requirements

    APCSP Requirements
    3a (i) Describe the overall use of this program:
    Because our group is full of foodies,
    we want to show our love and appreciation
    for different cuisines through our website.
    In this website, you can look through different
    cuisines in three Californian cities: San
    Fransisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego. These
    cuisines will have restaurant suggestions and
    reviews from online sources. There will be
    different restaurants information available
    on each page sorted by location all falling
    under different cuisines. There will be a
    database storing these restaurants that you
    can search through by cuisines and favorite
    your favorite food by logging in.
